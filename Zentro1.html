ZENTRO

Project Description.

This product was made in collaboration with artisans from the municipality of Tecozautla in the state of Hidalgo, México

Research.

The goal was to create objects as a focal point in any environment. Obsidian, a volcanic stone naturally formed in high temperature environments, is known for its elegant texture and dark, shiny color. México is known for being a rich source of obsidian, so together with the artisans incharge of the extraction and production of obsidian pieces, we worked to highlight the beauty of the material and adapt it to objects for everyday common spaces.

Development. Made of polished obsidian, stained open pore pine wood. A 3 mm brass base and a brass cup as an accessory element. This design fuses the natural beauty of volcanic stone with the warmth of wood and the elegance of brass, creating a unique piece that evokes the essence of this precious stone, transmitting its power and mystery through soft and curved lines. Obsidian, with its dark and shiny appearance, represents the strength and depth of the earth, while wood provides warmth and naturalness, symbolizing the connection with nature and tradition. On the other hand, brass adds a touch of sophistication and distinction, enhancing the beauty of the set.
Project Date. 2018.
